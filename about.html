Jiwon Kim
Works About
Jiwon Kim is a designer currently studying graphic design at ArtEZ University of the Arts in the Netherlands. She explores experimental graphic design based on research on materiality, structure, and visual perception, and her work mainly focuses on books and printed materials.

However, she does not see books as simple containers of content. Instead, she experiments with different binding and printing techniques, such as stitch binding, Japanese folding, and puff ink silkscreen. Through this, she looks at how form, structure, and the physical qualities of a medium affect the way people read and understand information. In doing so, she explores how graphic design can create new ways of seeing, reading, and experiencing content across different media.
