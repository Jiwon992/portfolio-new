Jiwon Kim
Works About
Jiwon Kim is a designer currently studying graphic design at ArtEZ University of the Arts in the Netherlands. Her work explores experimental graphic design based on research into materiality, structure, and visual perception, with a focus on books and printed media. She experiments with various binding and printing techniques, examining how the form, structure, and physical qualities of a medium influence the way information is read and understood. Through this process, she expands the possibilities of graphic design to create new ways of seeing, reading, and experiencing content across different media.

Her work begins from an interest in the past and the future, reflecting on social and environmental issues to encourage deeper thinking about the world.She works flexibly across media and methods, choosing the medium or approach that best conveys her ideas.
