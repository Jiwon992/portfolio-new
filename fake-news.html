Fake News on Facebook is a publication that collects and organizes fake news posted on Facebook. The posts contain no factual content and are categorized as left, right, or mainstream media based on political orientation. Some posts have been deleted, leaving only metadata such as when or where they were written.
Fake news is often removed after it has already spread widely, but the public perception it creates does not disappear easily. This publication functions not only as a collection of fake news, but also as a data archive that reveals how information is created, circulated, and removed in online spaces. By showing both the remaining data and the traces of deleted posts, the work invites readers to reconsider the reliability of the information we encounter and its influence.
