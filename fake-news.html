Fake News on Facebook is a publication that works with data from fake news posts on Facebook posts. The data is categorized into left, right, and mainstream sources, and all posts with no factual context. Each piece of data is visualized through color, size, and layout to represent its level of inaccuracy and political bias. By arranging the information this way, viewers can observe patterns, compare sources, and understand how misinformation spreads across the platform. The project aims to encourage critical thinking about online content and to highlight the ways in which false information can influence public perception.
