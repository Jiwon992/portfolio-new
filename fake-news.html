Fake News on Facebook is a publication based on a dataset of 390 fake news posts collected from Facebook. The book groups posts by political leaning - left, right, and mainstream sources and shows each post with a color that represents its political position. Information below each post shows where the data comes from.
Some posts have been deleted, so users can no longer see the original content. However, some information such as the source and date still remains. In these cases, the original title is replaced with the word “Facebook.”
Fake news is often deleted after it has already spread online. Even if the post disappears, the opinions or beliefs it created may still remain. This book presents the dataset in a repeated layout where only the title and color change, allowing readers to see how false information appears, spreads, and disappears
