Perspective Transition is a collaborative zine created with Naeun, developed from the idea that the way we see ourselves often differs from how others perceive us. To explore this contrast, we asked people to identify the most attractive part of our faces and then fold or tear our portraits based on their choices. Inspired by an artwork that addressed how individuals hide or erase parts of themselves, the project reflects how personal identity is shaped and reshaped through external viewpoints.
