This project is about the sorrow that rises in the moment you lose someone you love. The text silkscreened onto the window reflects my personal experience of losing my grandmother. When water falls onto the words, the letters blur, flow, and slowly disappear. The falling letters visually express emotions quietly collapsing — like moments I can no longer hold onto after losing someone I love.
