Raining is a window installation that visualizes the sadness that appears when losing someone I love. The text printed on the window with silkscreen reflects a personal experience of losing my grandmother. Because the text is printed directly on the glass, so when it rains, the letters blur, flows, and it slowly disappears. The letters that fall off the paint visually express the feeling of falling apart by myself, like a moment when I can no longer hold onto a loved one after losing it.
